Privacy Policy

Reely respects your privacy and is designed to work primarily on your device.

Information We Collect

Movie Library Data

Movies you mark as watched, add to your watchlist, rate, and your app preferences are stored locally on your device. This information is not shared with Reely.

Anonymous Analytics

Reely uses PostHog to collect anonymous usage information to help improve the app. This may include:

  • Features used within the app
  • Anonymous interaction events, such as opening Quick Fill or marking a movie as watched
  • App version and device information
  • Performance and diagnostic information

This information is used solely to improve Reely’s functionality, reliability, and user experience.

Information We Do Not Collect

Reely does not:

  • Require user accounts
  • Collect your name or email address
  • Sell your personal information
  • Use analytics for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Track your activity across other apps or websites

Data Sharing

No personal information is sold or shared with advertisers. Anonymous analytics data may be processed by PostHog only to help improve the app.

Third-Party Services

Reely uses The Movie Database (TMDB) for movie posters, metadata, and related movie information. Reely is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.

Reely also uses PostHog for anonymous analytics.

Data Security

Because your movie library and preferences are stored locally on your device, you maintain control over your data. While reasonable measures are taken to protect any analytics information collected, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

Reely is not intended to collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
